# Security Camera System App

This is the official app for the local security system server, designed to provide an easy-to-use interface for monitoring and managing your security camera system. The app seamlessly integrates with the server, allowing you to view live video feeds, receive security alerts, and perform various administrative tasks with just a few taps.

## Introduction

The Security Camera System App is a powerful companion to the local security system server, offering a user-friendly way to interact with your security setup. With this app, you can stay informed about potential threats and take immediate action when necessary, ensuring the safety of your premises at all times.

## Features

- **Live Video Feed**: View real-time video streams from your security cameras directly on your mobile device.
- **Security Alerts**: Receive instant notifications when potential threats or suspicious activities are detected.
- **Analytics**: Access detailed analytics and insights about your security system's performance, including alert rates and recent events.
- **Remote Control**: Start and stop monitoring, as well as perform administrative tasks like self-destruct sequences, right from your mobile device.
- **Intuitive Interface**: Enjoy a clean and intuitive user interface that makes navigating and managing your security system a breeze.

## Installation

The Security Camera System App is available for both iOS and Android devices. Follow these steps to install the app:

1. **iOS**: Open the App Store on your iOS device and search for "Security Camera System App". Tap the "Get" button to download and install the app.

2. **Android**: Open the Google Play Store on your Android device and search for "Security Camera System App". Tap the "Install" button to download and install the app.

Once the installation is complete, launch the app and follow the on-screen instructions to connect it to your local security system server.

## Usage

1. **Start Monitoring**: Tap the "Start Monitoring" button to begin receiving live video feeds and security alerts from your cameras.

2. **View Live Feed**: The app will display the live video feed from your security cameras. You can switch between different camera views if multiple cameras are connected.

3. **Receive Alerts**: If any suspicious activity or potential threats are detected, you will receive real-time alerts on your device. Tap on an alert to view more details and take appropriate action.

4. **Analytics**: Access detailed analytics about your security system's performance by tapping the "View Analytics" button. Here, you can view information such as the total number of images captured, images with security alerts, and the alert rate.

5. **Administrative Tasks**: Perform administrative tasks like stopping monitoring or initiating a self-destruct sequence by tapping the corresponding buttons in the app.
